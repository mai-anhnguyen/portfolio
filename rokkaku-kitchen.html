ABOUT US

We prepare each meal with care to satisfy your stomach and your heart. Enjoy the timeless atmosphere and cuisine in a traditional Kyoto townhouse.

First Floor

With the concept of being able to eat warm food any time of the day, for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, we offer rice, miso soup, a main dish, and side dishes in a casual set meal style. Enjoy a uniquely Kyoto experience in a slightly luxurious space overlooking the garden.

Second Floor

In the Japanese-style room on the second floor, we hold elegant Kyoto-style events such as small group tea ceremonies and photo sessions with maiko. Event information will be posted on our website from time to time.
